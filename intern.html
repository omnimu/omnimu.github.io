Our Mission

The Society is an international, multidisciplinary scientific association devoted to research on psychotherapy. The aims of the Society are to:

  • encourage the development of scientific research on psychotherapy
  • foster the communication, understanding, and use of research findings
  • enhance the scientific and social value of psychotherapy research
  • contribute, through research, to enhancing the effectiveness of psychotherapies.
